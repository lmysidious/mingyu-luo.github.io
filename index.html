About

I am a postdoc at the Department of Ecology and Evolutioanry Biology, UCLA. My work focuses on theory of ecosystem stability, coarse-graining of ecological models, temporal dynamics of ecological systems, and other topics in theoretical and statistical ecology. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Chuliang Song.
